How to play

You are Coral. Rival species spread across the same living-chemistry field. Hold the most territory to clear each level — they get tougher as you climb.

  • Left-drag the field to seed your pattern; it grows and claims ground.
  • Seeding spends nutrient (top-left bar), which refills faster the more territory you hold — an early lead snowballs.
  • You can't paint everywhere — pick your spots. Seed open ground ahead of the fronts to out-claim the AI.
  • Brush S / M / L — bigger stamp, more nutrient.
  • Clear a level by holding the most territory when time runs out (or reaching the win %). Higher levels add faster — then more — opponents.
  • R new game  ·  Space next level  ·  ⚙ settings.

Bloom needs WebGPU

This game simulates a million-cell chemistry field on the GPU with compute shaders. Your browser doesn't expose WebGPU, so it can't run.

Use a recent Chrome or Edge (WebGPU on by default), or Firefox / Safari 18+. On desktop, make sure hardware acceleration is enabled.
